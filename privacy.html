Privacy Policy

Sector Defense  •  Effective Date: March 20, 2026  •  Last Updated: March 20, 2026

Plain-language summary: Sector Defense does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Gameplay progress and settings are stored locally on your device only. No accounts. No analytics. No servers receiving your data.

Section 01

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Helix Games Studio ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the mobile game Sector Defense (the "Game"), available on Apple's App Store for iOS devices.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Because of the way Sector Defense is designed, we collect virtually no information about you. Please read this policy to understand what we do and do not collect.

Section 02

Information We Collect

Sector Defense stores the following data locally on your device using your device's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server:

  • Gameplay progress (levels completed, scores, unlocked content)
  • Game settings and preferences (audio, display options)
  • Cosmetic purchase records (which hero skins, tower skins, and map themes you have unlocked)
  • Helix Credits balance (virtual currency, stored locally)

This data exists solely on your device to save your game state between sessions. It is not associated with any account, identifier, or personal information.

Section 03

Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be explicit about what we do not collect:

  • No name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information (PII)
  • No device identifiers, advertising IDs, or IP addresses
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics, session data, or behavioral tracking
  • No crash reports or diagnostic telemetry sent to our servers
  • No user accounts or authentication credentials
  • No social graph, contacts, or communications
  • No camera, microphone, or other sensor data

Sector Defense does not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or data collection frameworks.

Section 04

In-App Purchases and Apple's App Store

Sector Defense offers in-app purchases for cosmetic items (hero skins, tower skins, and map themes) and premium virtual currency (Helix Credits) through Apple's App Store In-App Purchase (IAP) system.

All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple. We do not receive, store, or process your payment card details, billing address, or any other financial information. Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's own Privacy Policy, available at apple.com/legal/privacy.

We receive only a confirmation from Apple that a purchase was completed, which we use to unlock the corresponding in-game content on your device. This confirmation does not include your payment details.

Section 05

Third-Party Services

Sector Defense does not use any third-party services that collect or process personal data, with the sole exception of Apple's App Store for in-app purchase processing (described above).

The Game uses the following open-source font resources, loaded from Google Fonts at startup:

  • JetBrains Mono — SIL Open Font License 1.1
  • Inter — SIL Open Font License 1.1

Font loading may involve a network request to Google's servers. Google's privacy practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy. No game-specific or personal data is transmitted in connection with font loading.

Section 06

Data Retention and Deletion

All game data is stored locally on your device. You have full control over this data:

  • Clearing app data: Deleting the Sector Defense app from your device removes all locally stored game data permanently.
  • No server-side data: Because we do not store any data on our servers, there is no additional deletion request necessary on our end.
  • No backups: We do not maintain backups of your game data. If you delete the app, your progress cannot be recovered by us.
Section 07

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Sector Defense is rated 9+ on the Apple App Store for mild fantasy violence. The Game is not directed at children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Because the Game collects no personal information from any user, there is no special risk to younger users. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has somehow provided personal information through the Game, please contact us — though we have no mechanism to receive such data.

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the App Store's guidelines for apps available to users under 13.

Section 08

GDPR — Users in the European Economic Area

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or equivalent laws may apply to the processing of your personal data.

Because Sector Defense does not collect, process, or store any personal data on our systems, there is no personal data processing for which we act as a data controller under GDPR. All game data is stored locally on your device and is entirely within your control.

There is no need to submit a data subject access request, erasure request, or portability request, as we hold no personal data about you. If you have questions, please contact us at the address below.

Section 09

CCPA — California Residents

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) may provide you with certain rights regarding your personal information.

Helix Games Studio does not sell, share, or monetize personal information. We do not collect personal information from Sector Defense players. As a result:

  • There is no personal information to disclose, delete, or correct.
  • We do not sell or share personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • No opt-out mechanism is required because no data sale or sharing occurs.

For any privacy inquiries, California residents may contact us using the details in Section 11.

Section 10

Security

Because all game data is stored locally on your device, the security of that data is governed by your device's own security features (such as device passcode or biometric lock). We do not transmit your data and therefore cannot be responsible for its security in transit or on external servers — because neither exists.

We encourage you to use your device's built-in security features to protect your personal device and any data stored on it.
